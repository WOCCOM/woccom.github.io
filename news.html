Workshop Announcement: Computational Mathematics and Mathematical Modelling 2026

Posted: April 2026

The WOCCOM Research Group is proud to announce the upcoming workshop on Computational Mathematics and Mathematical Modelling.

Date: June 29 – 03 July 2026
Venue: University of KwaZulu-Natal, Pietermaritzburg

The workshop will focus on Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Optimization. It will serve as a platform for collaboration between researchers, industry experts, and students from Africa and the world.

For more information, please visit the Workshop 2026 page.
